# hae

DEFINITION / ENGLISH: -er / one who …
PART OF SPEECH: affix
NOTES: added as a suffix to verbs (after removing “sii”) to turn it into a noun that does that action. i.e. leiv (leiv%206921c137c5ec4224854319a6575e1d97.md) (dream) + hae (hae%201f8c5cb610f5807db7ecd40f90b9f704.md) (-er) → leivhae (leivhae%201f8c5cb610f58069b0acc9ed0b8282e5.md) (dreamer)
