Merisdae's headband, crafted with 5 pieces of moon rock along the top, one piece from each moon. Imbued with soul pieces of [[CHARACTERS/Wefa/index\|Wefa]], [[CHARACTERS/Anwi/index\|Anwi]], and [[CHARACTERS/Esela/index\|Esela]].

![[ARTIFACTS/Moonband/moonband.png|500]]

It was originally crafted by Anwi and Wefa in order to construct Merisdae. Then, it was repaired by Esela.
